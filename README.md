## Why This App Exists

I created Tiyoweh to support mindful practice on Apple Watch in a way that is **quiet, flexible, and intentional**. The app provides a simple timer that works across a wide range of activities while accurately recording **Mindful Minutes** in Apple Health. Corresponding sessions are also recorded in the Apple Workout app, allowing mindfulness and movement to sit naturally alongside daily activity.

The Apple Watch experience is intentionally minimal, designed to support presence rather than distraction. A companion iPhone app extends this experience with customisation, goals, and gentle reminders ‚Äî without pulling attention away from practice.
