Any information presented within the App reflects personal views and is subject to change at any time.

2. Information Collection

Tiyoweh does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with any external servers, advertisers, analytics providers, or third parties.

  • No user accounts are required
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No cookies are used
  • No advertising is included

All data remains on your device or within Apple’s secure systems.

3. HealthKit Data (Apple Health)

If you choose to grant permission, Tiyoweh may access health-related data via Apple HealthKit.

Health Data Accessed

Tiyoweh may request permission to read:

  • Mindful Minutes
  • Activity data
  • Active Energy
  • Heart Rate

Tiyoweh may also request permission to write data to Apple Health to save Tiyoweh sessions.

How Health Data Is Used

  • Health data is used only within the App to display summaries and session history
  • Health data is not transmitted or stored on external servers
  • Health data is not shared with third parties
  • Health data is not used for advertising, analytics, or profiling

HealthKit data is never sold or shared, in accordance with Apple’s HealthKit policies.

User Control

  • Granting Health access is optional
  • Permissions can be changed or revoked at any time in the Apple Health app
  • All stored data is deleted when the App is removed from your device

4. CarPlay

When used with Apple CarPlay, Tiyoweh only displays information already available within the App to support safe, glanceable interaction.

  • No additional data is collected when using CarPlay
  • No data is transmitted outside the device
  • CarPlay access is display-only and follows Apple’s CarPlay guidelines

5. Third-Party Services

Tiyoweh does not use third-party SDKs or services that collect personal or health information.
